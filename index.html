AboutUs

Why Choose us?

Our restaurant is cosy , elegant and friendly.Is really a way to feel at home. You will be served by a very high and qualified waiters who are able to fulfill the requests of the more demanding clients. Our cuisine is typically mediterranean and we pay a very great attention on the quality and freshness of the products that we select in the nearby villages.And it is the taste,the Mediterranean taste to make it special, because the dishes are all “homemade” from the queen of the menu:Albanian Meat. Our family has a butcher shop since 2010 and this is what makes possible the freshness of our products daily.
